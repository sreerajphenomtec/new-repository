Vinita's story

Vinita(23, Delhi) is the eldest of her 3 siblings. She lives in West Delhi with her mother, two younger sisters and a brother. She is currently pursuing her Bachelors in Education (B.Ed) to fulfil her mother’s dream to see her become a teacher. Vinita is supporting her family by working as a front desk executive for 2 years alongside her education and volunteering role at Indus Action.

Vinita’s journey began with Indus Action in 2017 as a Shiksha Sahyogi, to volunteer for Right to Education. In the last four years, Vinita went on to become a Champion in every sense as she supported several parents and children to rightfully seek awareness and admission. She joined Samaaj 3.5% program as a Community Champion and actively worked on the campaign with Children with Special Needs (CWSN) under RTE. She also managed a team of 5 women and conducted on-ground surveys, calling and admission camps to assist parents and children.

Vinita juggled her mother’s dream, aspirations, formal education, a full time job and a volunteer role with Indus Action with spirit and resilience, which also happen to be her significant learnings from the journey. Her dream is to continue working on CWSN awareness with organizations and pursue her studies to become a teacher.
